Predicting Economic Recessions

Using yield curve inversion as a predictive indicator.

Project Overview

This project analyzes the powerful relationship between the **yield curve spread** and economic recessions.

Historically, an inverted yield curve—where short-term bond yields exceed long-term yields—has consistently preceded economic downturns. This model processes historical bond data to identify and visualize the specific spreads with the highest predictive correlation.
